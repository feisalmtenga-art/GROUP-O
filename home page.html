Group Introduction

We are the BSc Business Information Technology (BIT) cohort at the College of Information and Communication Technologies (COICT), University of Dar es Salaam. This platform is our collective digital home a space where each of our 80 students showcases their individual academic journey, technical projects, and professional aspirations.

Our programme sits at the intersection of computer science and business management, equipping us with the knowledge to design, develop, and manage information systems that drive realworld impact. From database design and software development to ebusiness strategy and systems analysis, we are trained to think both as technologists and as business strategists.

This portfolio hub represents not just our coursework, but our growth the skills we have built, the problems we have solved, and the professionals we are becoming. Browse through our individual profiles and discover the unique strengths, projects, and stories that make up our class.
